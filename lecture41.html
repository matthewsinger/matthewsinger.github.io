Accumulators

The four questions of an accumulator:

  1. What does it represent?

  2. Initial value?

  3. How to update between calls?

  4. How to use in final answer?

Design sum/acc, reverse, and foldl. Discuss the concept of tail-recursion; emphasize not all accumulators lead to tail recursion. Then, build a tail-recursive build-list and argmax.
